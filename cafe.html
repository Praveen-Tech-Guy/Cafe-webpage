Our Story

Crafted with Care

We partner with small farms and roast in micro-batches to bring out nuanced flavors. Our space is designed with acoustic comfort, warm lighting, and tactile materials — a retreat in the heart of the city.

  • Single-origin beans & seasonal blends
  • Slow bar: pour-over, siphon, Chemex
